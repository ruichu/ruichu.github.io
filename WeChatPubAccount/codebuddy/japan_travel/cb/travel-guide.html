Recommended Proposal Spots

1. Maruyama Park, Kyoto (Top Recommendation)

Perfect for an evening proposal under the illuminated cherry blossoms. The park's famous weeping cherry tree creates a magical atmosphere, especially when lit up at night.

  • Best timing: Evening of Day 4
  • Location: Central Kyoto
  • Atmosphere: Romantic, traditional Japanese setting

2. Mount Fuji Viewing Point (Alternative)

For a dramatic backdrop, consider proposing with Mount Fuji in view during your Day 7 trip to Hakone.

3. Philosopher's Path, Kyoto (Backup)

A peaceful stone path lined with cherry trees, perfect for a private moment away from crowds.

Photography Tips

Cherry Blossom Photography
  • Best lighting: Early morning or late afternoon (golden hour)
  • Use aperture priority mode (f/2.8-f/5.6) for bokeh effect
  • Try both close-ups and wide shots
  • Include people for scale and context
  • Check weather forecast for best shooting conditions
Proposal Photo Tips
  • Consider hiring a local photographer
  • Scout location in advance
  • Plan for sunset/golden hour timing
  • Have a friend ready with camera if possible
  • Remember to capture reaction shots
  • Take photos from multiple angles
